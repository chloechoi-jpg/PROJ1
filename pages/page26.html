Final Silence (Ending)

Your body grows still. Breath fades. Thought dissolves. There is no pain—only a deep, endless quiet. But silence does not last. Awareness slowly returns, not to warmth or light, but to space. You feel yourself standing, weightless yet solid, as if you never truly left. Before you stretches a field of silver grass beneath a vast, waiting sky. Whatever ended did not stop you. The dream continues.
